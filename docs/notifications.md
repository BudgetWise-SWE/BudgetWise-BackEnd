# Notification System

The Notification module manages the dispatch and status tracking of system-generated alerts and user messages.

## Notification Classifications

- **Budgetary Alerts**: Dispatched when spending reaches or exceeds predefined thresholds.
- **Objective Milestones**: Generated upon the successful completion of a savings goal.
- **System Communications**: Platform-wide announcements or account-specific administrative messages.

## Resource Management

Users can retrieve a complete history of notifications and modify the read status of individual or bulk records through the provided API endpoints.
